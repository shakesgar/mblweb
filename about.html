About Us

Mortgage Brain was launched in the UK in 1986 in order to provide mortgage intermediaries with a simple way to view and illustrate all mortgage products available in the market. In 2001 the company was purchased by a consortium of lenders to develop the first electronic trading platform.

Mortgage Brain entered into the Irish Market in 2003 with a fully customised solution for Irish mortgage intermediaries, lenders and legislation. Mortgage Brain team works side by side with mortgage intermediary representative groups and currently enjoys a strong link with the Independent Mortgage Advisors Federation.

Today, Mortgage Brain provides the most accurate mortgage quotation system and the most proficient compliance process in the industry. Mortgage Brain is also integrated with a number of lenders, which is continuing to grow, and sets the standard for Irelands common trading platform.
